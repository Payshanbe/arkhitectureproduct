# Portfolio Plan

Version 1.0 flagship project plan for the architecture studio website.

These eight projects define the initial editorial portfolio. They are realistic demonstration projects designed to support the studio positioning: calm architecture, material clarity, atmosphere, and restraint.

## Portfolio Direction

The portfolio should feel balanced across residential, interiors, hospitality, and cultural work.

The sequence should show range without feeling scattered:

1. A flagship residence.
2. A refined apartment interior.
3. A gallery or cultural space.
4. A small hospitality project.
5. A landscape-connected house.
6. A material-led interior.
7. A coastal or courtyard project.
8. A compact retreat or pavilion.

## Project 01 - Courtyard Residence

Title: Courtyard Residence

Location: Paros, Greece

Year: 2026

Type: Residential

Area: 420 sqm

Services:

- Architecture
- Interior Design
- Landscape

Architectural concept:

A low residence organized around a protected courtyard, using stone walls, shaded thresholds, and deep openings to create a quiet relationship between interior life and the Aegean climate.

Visual direction:

- Warm limestone.
- White plaster.
- Olive trees.
- Soft morning light.
- Long horizontal compositions.
- Courtyard shadows and open thresholds.

## Project 05 - Ridge House

Title: Ridge House

Location: Sonoma, United States

Year: 2025

Type: Residential

Area: 510 sqm

Services:

- Architecture
- Interior Design
- Master Planning
- Landscape

Architectural concept:

A hillside residence that follows the contour of the land, using a long roofline, protected terraces, and a restrained material palette to frame the valley without dominating it.

Visual direction:

- Concrete base.
