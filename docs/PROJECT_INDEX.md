# Project Index

Overview of the eight flagship projects prepared for Version 1.0 editorial content and future CMS entry.

## Portfolio Summary

The portfolio presents a restrained architecture and interiors studio through eight distinct projects. Each project belongs to the same philosophy of light, material, atmosphere, and context, while varying by scale, geography, and spatial character.

## Editorial Distinction

Courtyard Residence:

- Defines the studio's core language of courtyard, stone, shade, and domestic calm.

Stone Apartment:

- Shows refined interior discipline through stone, oak, and proportion.

North Light Gallery:

- Establishes cultural credibility through light control and spatial restraint.

Limestone Hotel:

- Expands the portfolio into hospitality without losing the studio's quiet tone.

Ridge House:

- Demonstrates landscape-led residential architecture and larger-scale planning.

Oak Room:

- Shows the studio's ability to make small interiors feel architectural.

Plaster House:

- Introduces tropical climate, mass, and garden thresholds.

Garden Pavilion:

- Concludes the portfolio with a small, precise architecture of pause and stillness.
