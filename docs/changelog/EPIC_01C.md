## Visual Direction

The drawing now follows a Courtyard House system:

- grid and reference lines;
- site boundary and orientation;
- courtyard envelope;
- walls, columns, and volumes;
- subtle material hatching;
- directional light and shadow;
- refined final atmosphere.
